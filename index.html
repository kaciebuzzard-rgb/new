About Kacie

Kacie Wielgus (Buzzard) is a speaker, writer, and inclusion consultant whose work lives at the intersection of lived experience and systems change.

As a mother of a child with a rare genetic condition, a caregiver navigating complex medical and educational systems, and a communications strategist with nearly two decades of professional experience, she brings both expertise and earned perspective to every room she enters.

Kacie speaks and writes about human-centered communication, invisible labor, advocacy, neurodiversity, and the structural realities families face behind closed doors. Her work blends strategy with story — helping organizations move beyond performative inclusion into meaningful action.

What sets her apart

  • ✔ Nearly 20 years in communications & digital strategy
  • ✔ Published author & co-author
  • ✔ Nationally engaged disability advocate
  • ✔ Experienced keynote & workshop facilitator
  • ✔ Lived experience navigating healthcare & education systems
