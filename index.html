About Kacie

Kacie Wielgus (Buzzard) is a speaker, writer, and inclusion consultant whose work lives at the intersection of lived experience and systems change.

As a mother of a child with a rare genetic condition and a communications strategist with nearly two decades of experience, she brings both expertise and earned perspective to every room she enters.

Her work blends strategy with story — helping organizations move beyond performative inclusion into meaningful action.

What Sets Her Apart

  • Nearly 20 years in communications & digital strategy
  • Published author & co-author
  • National disability advocate
  • Experienced keynote & workshop facilitator
  • Lived experience navigating healthcare & education systems
